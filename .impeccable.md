## Design Context

### Users
ThreadCraft users are content creators, influencers, and marketers who want to quickly turn video content (YouTube/Instagram) into high-quality X (Twitter) threads. They value efficiency but also expect a professional, refined presentation for their output.

### Brand Personality
Refined, Timeless, Artisanal, Sophisticated, Classic. The tone is "Elegant". It should feel like a premium tool for serious craftsmen, not a generic "AI" utility.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Luxury/Refined. Subtle ivory backgrounds, rich black text, and gold accents.
- **Theme**: Light-mode only (Soft Ivory base).
- **Typography**: Editorial feel with Playfair Display (Headings), Lora (Body), and DM Sans (Functional labels).
- **Anti-references**: Low-contrast "developer" dark modes, generic purple/blue gradients, rounded "bubbly" buttons.

### Design Principles
1. **Sophisticated Restraint**: Use generous white space and avoids visual clutter.
2. **Artisanal Quality**: Every detail (spacing, hierarchy, typography) should feel intentional and handcrafted.
3. **Understated Luxury**: Use the secondary ivory palette and gold accent sparingly but effectively.
4. **Editorial Hierarchy**: Prioritize readability and high-contrast typography to make generated content feel like published literature.
